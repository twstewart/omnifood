How It Works

Your Daily Dose of Health in Three Simple Steps

1

Tell us what you like

Never again waste time thinking about what to eat! Omnifood AI will create a 100% personalized weekly meal plan just for you. It makes sure you get all the nutrients and vitamins you need, no matter what diet you follow!

Once per week you will have a chance to approve the meal plan generated for you by Omnifood AI. You can change the ingredients, swap entire meals, or even add your own recipes!

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of our FAQs

  • How does Omnifood's meal delivery service work?

    Our meal delivery service allows you to skip meal planning and grocery shopping. Omnifood delivers step-by-step recipes and fresh, pre-portioned ingredients right to your door. First, you set your meal plan preferences. You'll select from a wide variety of delicious weekly recipes carefully arranged by our top chefs.

  • Which food meal plans and recipes does Omnifood offer?

    Omnifood has the most variety in terms of recipes from meat & veggies, to low-carbohydrate and low-calorie options, and more. You can choose from three delicious meal plan options. With Omnifood, you have the power to choose according to your preference and lifestyle. Our low-calorie meal plans feature nutritionist-approved recipes that range between 500 to 750 calories.

  • How many times a week does Omnifood deliver?

    Once a week. You can expect your meal kits to arrive between 8 am to 8 pm. We deliver anywhere in the United States, except Alaska and Hawaii.

  • Does Omnifood give nutrition and calorie information?

    Simply put,yes! We promise that every single one of our meal kits is appropriately marked. They have all the nutritional facts you are looking for. Things like calorie content and the amount of carbohydrates, protein, dietary fiber, sugar, sodium, unsaturated and saturated fat, and cholesterol.

  • Does Omnifood promote a healthy lifestyle?

    That's one of the reasons we started this company. To promote a healthy lifestyle. Omnifood offers a wide array of flavorful and nutritious meal plan menu options that cater to a range of dietary needs, making use of tasty, seasonal vegetables and other fresh produce.

  • If something comes up can I skip a week of delivery?

    Absolutely. The way Omnifood's meal subscription service works, you are always in control. If you do not want to receive a meal kit on a particular week, you do not have to. To avoid charges when skipping a week, you simply just have to “pause” your meal subscription order or cancel your meal subscription after logging into your account. Pausing or canceling your meal delivery orders should be done by 11:59 pm PST, five days before your next delivery schedule. Note that you will be charged on all orders that you failed to cancel before the cut-off date.
