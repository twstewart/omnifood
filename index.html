How it works

A Much Improved Diet with Three Simple Steps

1.

Start by telling us what you like

Never again waste time trying to decide what to eat! Omnifood AI is capable of creating a 100% personalized weekly meal plan for you. Our AI will make sure you get all the nutrients and vitamins you need, no matter which diet you follow!

2.

Approve your weekly meal plan

Once per week you will have a chance to approve the meal plan that has been generated for you by Omnifood AI. You will have the option of changing the ingredients, swapping entire meals, or even adding your own recipes!

3.

Delivered at a convenient time

The best chefs in town will cook your selected meal everyday, and we will deliver it to your door at whatever time that works best for you. If something comes up, you are able to change the delivery date as well as the mailing address!

FAQs

Here are some of our most frequently asked questions

  • How does Omnifood's meal delivery service work?

    Our meal delivery service allows you to skip meal planning and grocery shopping. Omnifood delivers step-by-step recipes and fresh, pre-portioned ingredients right to your door. First, you set your meal plan preferences. You'll select from a wide variety of delicious weekly recipes carefully arranged by our top chefs.

  • Which food meal plans and recipes does Omnifood offer?

    Omnifood has the most variety in terms of recipes from meat & veggies, to low-carbohydrate and low-calorie options, and more. You can choose from three delicious meal plan options. With Omnifood, you have the power to choose according to your preference and lifestyle. Our low-calorie meal plans feature nutritionist-approved recipes that range between 400 to 750 calories.

  • How many times a week does Omnifood deliver?

    Once a week. You can expect your meal kits to arrive between 8 am to 8 pm. We deliver anywhere in the United States, except Alaska and Hawaii.

  • Does Omnifood give nutrition and calorie information?

    Simply put,yes! We promise that every single one of our meal kits is appropriately marked. They have all the nutritional facts you are looking for so you can keep track of your caloric intake. Things like calorie content and the amount of carbohydrates, protein, dietary fiber, sugar, sodium, unsaturated and saturated fat, and cholesterol etc.

  • Does Omnifood promote a healthy lifestyle?

    That's one of the reasons we started this company. To promote a healthy lifestyle. Omnifood offers a wide variety of flavorful and nutritious food options that cater to a range of dietary needs, making use of fresh, seasonal produce and ingredients that we source our from local farms.

  • If something comes up can I skip a week of delivery?

    Absolutely. The way Omnifood's meal subscription service works, you are always in control. If you don't want to receive a meal kit during a particular week, skip it. To avoid charges when skipping a week, you simply just have to "pause" your meal subscription order or cancel your meal subscription after logging into your account. Pausing or canceling your meal delivery orders should be done by 11:59 pm PST, five days before your next delivery schedule. Note that you will be charged on all orders that you failed to pause or cancel before the cut-off date.
