How it works

A Much Improved Diet with Three Simple Steps

1.

Start by telling us what you like

Never again waste time trying to decide what to eat! Omnifood AI is capable of creating a 100% personalized weekly meal plan for you. Our AI will make sure you get all the nutrients and vitamins you need, no matter which diet you follow!

2.

Approve your weekly meal plan

Once per week you will have a chance to approve the meal plan that has been generated for you by Omnifood AI. You will have the option of changing the ingredients, swapping entire meals, or even adding your own recipes!

3.

Delivered at a convenient time

The best chefs in town will cook your selected meal everyday, and we will deliver it to your door at whatever time that works best for you. If something comes up, you are able to change the delivery date as well as the mailing address!
